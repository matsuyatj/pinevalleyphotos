    I'm an amateur photographer based in south Louisiana, specifically the Baton Rouge area, though I go wherever the photos take me. I'm an IT professional by trade, which keeps me pretty busy, but I take time for my photography whenever I can. My passion for photography started in middle school, where I was fortunate enough to be able to take a short photography class. From that moment, I was hooked. Ever since, I've tried to develop my skills; trying to find the right frame for a setting, or just the right angle continues to fascinate me to this day as much as it did when I was a kid. I've had experience photographing weddings, graduations, newborns, and more.


    I love traveling to new places and taking in the sights, local cuisine, and culture, and capturing as much of it on film as I can. If you're interested in my photos, or would like me to do a photoshoot for you, don't hesitate to reach out! I'm always up for a new challenge, and look forward to helping you capture memories that'll last for a lifetime.
